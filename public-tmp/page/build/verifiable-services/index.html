Self Sovereign Services

With Verana, organizations and creators can deploy self-sovereign verifiable services and stay in full control of identity, data, and distribution.

Why Verifiable Services Matter

Owning a Verifiable Service transforms how you interact with digital ecosystems

Full Data Ownership

You control your data and connections. No third parties can access, manipulate, or monetize your information without consent.

Privacy by Design

No intermediaries mining user interactions. Built-in privacy protections keep communications confidential.

Proof of Authenticity

Prove ownership of your service to your audience through cryptographic verification that can’t be faked.

Signed Content

Integrate with C2PA so every piece of content is cryptographically signed by your DID, preventing deepfakes.

Deployment Freedom
