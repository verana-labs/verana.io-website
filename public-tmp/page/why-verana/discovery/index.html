Why Discoverability is Broken

Today, consumer discoverability of goods and services is controlled by centralized platforms that rely on advertising, opaque SEO algorithms, and now even sponsored AI results.

Wealth Concentration

Wealth is concentrated in a handful of dominant companies that control discovery algorithms.

Stifled Innovation

Startups and SMEs cannot compete with massive advertising budgets, limiting innovation.

Higher Prices

Intermediaries capture disproportionate value, forcing consumers to pay higher prices.

Service Discovery: Re-distribution of Wealth

Verana replaces the advertising monopoly with an open, decentralized, and verifiable trust system powered by verifiable credentials.

Any ecosystem can certify claims, service owners attach those credentials to their digital channels, and the Trust Resolver indexes the proofs automatically.

Ecosystems

Establish decentralized trust networks that specialize in certifying claims for specific industries or jurisdictions.

Credentials

Organizations and individuals obtain verifiable credentials they can attach to every service endpoint.

Auto-Indexing

Credentials are crawled, cryptographically verified, and added to the global directory of verifiable data.

Discovery

Services surface because of the claims they can prove—not because of who bought ads.

Economic Transformation

Verana shifts power away from centralized platforms toward a federated economy of ecosystems that compete on trust, authenticity, and credential quality.
