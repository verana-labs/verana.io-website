Team

The visionaries and technologists building the future of digital trust and sovereignty.

Our Values

The principles that guide our mission to build a more trustworthy and sovereign digital world.

Openness

We believe in open standards, open source, and transparency. Our protocols are public, auditable, and designed for interoperability.

Sovereignty

Individuals and organizations should have full control over their digital identities, data, and services without platform dependencies.

Innovation

We push the boundaries of what's possible with cryptography, distributed systems, and decentralized networks to solve real-world problems.

Chronology

Verana's journey is rooted in solving the three systemic problems of the internet — trust, ownership, and discovery.

Genesis

2020

After many sessions at the Internet Identity Workshop (IIW), Fabrice Rochette and Ariel Gentile invent the concept of Verifiable Trust.

Verifiable Trust Specification
Verifiable Public Registry Specification

First Proofs of Concept

2021–2022

Development of the first Verifiable User Agent, the Hologram Messaging App. Creation of Verifiable Service chatbots, demonstrating that the Verifiable Trust model works in practice.

Hologram.zone launched

Network Development

2023

Mathieu Gauthron joins to lead the build of the first decentralized, blockchain-based Verifiable Public Registry (VPR) implementation.

Team expansion begins

Verana Emerges

2024

The project adopts its permanent name: Verana. Expansion of the ecosystem vision around Verifiable Services and Trust Registries.

Brand identity established
Ecosystem vision expanded

Testnet & Strategic Growth

2025

Philip A. Bildner joins as Strategic Advisor to guide governance, fundraising, and partnerships. Launch of the Verana Network Testnet.

Strategic advisor onboarded
Testnet launched
