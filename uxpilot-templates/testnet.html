Getting Started with the Testnet

1

Install a Cosmos SDK Wallet

Install a cosmos SDK wallet, such as Keplr, Leap, or any interchain-ts compatible wallet.

Keplr

Download

Leap

Download

Other Wallets

View Full List
2

Access the Frontend

Access the frontend, connect your wallet to obtain your Verana address. You can copy the address by clicking the copy button on the top right corner.

3

Download Hologram Messaging

Download Hologram Messaging with your cellphone to interact with verifiable services.

Download Hologram
4

Connect to the Faucet

Connect to the faucet. When connected to the faucet service, just write /to <your verana address> to get 100 VNA.

Scan to connect to faucet

5

Execute Transactions

You can now interact with the frontend and execute transactions on the Verana network.
