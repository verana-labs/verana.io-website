Services

Deploy verifiable services that you fully control, from global organizations to individual creators.

With Verana, anyone, from global organizations to individual creators, can deploy their own verifiable service (VS). Unlike centralized platforms, verifiable services are self-sovereign, meaning you fully control your data, identity, and audience relationships.

Why Verifiable Services Matter

Owning a Verifiable Service transforms how you interact with digital ecosystems

Full Data Ownership

You control your data and connections. No third parties can access, manipulate, or monetize your information without your explicit consent.

Privacy by Design

No intermediaries exploiting or mining user interactions. Built-in privacy protection ensures your communications remain confidential.

Proof of Authenticity

Prove ownership of your service to your audience through cryptographic verification that can't be faked or stolen.

Signed Content

Integrate with C2PA so all published content is cryptographically signed by your DID, ensuring authenticity and preventing deepfakes.

Deployment Freedom

Host anywhere: cloud, self-hosted, or migrate freely at any time. No vendor lock-in or platform dependencies.
