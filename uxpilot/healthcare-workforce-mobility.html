The Problem: Fragmented Verification

Healthcare workers are the backbone of public health systems. Yet, their mobility across hospitals, clinics, and cities is severely constrained by duplicated, manual, and siloed verification processes.

The Impact

This inefficiency translates into wasted time, wasted resources, and slower access to healthcare services for patients.

Current Challenges

Surgeons fully vetted in one city must undergo same checks elsewhere
Onboarding verifies identity, licenses, grants, DBS checks, training records
Each verification is costly, time-consuming, and delays work
Hospitals repeat checks other trusted institutions performed

Solution: Healthcare Workforce Ecosystem

The government (or national healthcare authority) establishes a Healthcare Workforce Ecosystem, a digital trust framework for managing verification and compliance across all participants.

Establish Ecosystem and Governance Framework

  • The Healthcare Workforce Ecosystem defines and publishes an Ecosystem Governance Framework (EGF)
  • The EGF sets policies for credential issuance, verification, revocation, and compliance
  • Ensures consistent standards nationwide

Create an Ecosystem Trust Registry

The ecosystem deploys a Trust Registry on Verana to implement the EGF rules.

Manages:

List of authorized issuers and verifiers

Approve Issuers and Verifiers

Issuers

Universities, professional councils, certification authorities, and government agencies can apply to issue credentials.

Once validated, they become authorized issuers

Verifiers

Hospitals, clinics, research centers, and government bodies can apply for verifier status.

Allows them to request and validate credential presentations

This ensures that only accredited entities can issue or verify credentials within the ecosystem.

Digital Wallet

Selective Disclosure

Share only necessary claims while preserving privacy

Key Features:

Healthcare workers receive verifiable credentials from trusted issuers (universities, licensing boards, training organizations)
Credentials are stored in a Digital Wallet (a Verifiable User Agent) controlled solely by the worker
Workers can share only the necessary claims (e.g., proof of valid license, specialty, training)
Uses selective disclosure, preserving privacy while proving eligibility
