Why Discoverability is Broken

Today, consumer discoverability of goods and services is controlled by centralized platforms that rely on advertising, opaque SEO algorithms, and now even sponsored AI results.

Wealth Concentration

Wealth is concentrated in a handful of dominant companies that control discovery algorithms.

Stifled Innovation

Startups and SMEs cannot compete with massive advertising budgets, limiting innovation.

Higher Prices

Intermediaries capture disproportionate value, forcing consumers to pay higher prices.

Service Discovery: Re-distribution of Wealth

Verana replaces this extractive model with an open, decentralized, and verifiable trust system powered by verifiable credentials:

Ecosystems

Establish decentralized trust networks, specializing in certifying specific claims within defined jurisdictions.

Credentials

Individuals and organizations collect verifiable credentials that can be attached to their digital channels.

Auto-Indexing

Credentials are crawled, verified, and included in a global directory of verifiable data.

Discovery

Services are surfaced based on what their credentials prove, not on who paid for ads.

Economic Transformation

This marks a shift away from centralized control by a few corporations toward a federated economy of thousands of ecosystems, each competing on trust, authenticity, and the quality of their certifications.
